Big in Lights - an electro powerhouse hailing from Bristol - combine powerful vocals with compelling synthesizers, melodic sax riffs and killer bass lines. Comprising of vocalist Amanda Hug n Kiss, vocalist and guitarist Brock Freeway, saxophonist Devon Heartbeat, bassist Papa Goldlove and DJ Chrissy Hotpants, Big in Lights distil a wide range of musical influences ranging from soul, jazz and blues to hip hop, funk and disco. Described by BBC Introducing as 'like somebody has cryogenically frozen Quincy Jones and then forced him to mate with Prince in the future' these guys are ones to watch.
